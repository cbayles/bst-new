We have reservations for rooms and tee times on Friday, October 17th through Tuesday, October 21st.

 Here is the line up:

 Fly into Vegas on Friday the 17th, Drive to Mesquite where we are booked at the Oasis Resort, our rooms are in the Manor section of the hotel, these are the newer rooms across the street from the casino and connected by a skywalk. Also relatively close to Casa Blanca (which is completely booked with a Long Drive Contest participants).

 Currently all tee times are reserved for the first 16 players to sign up and send a check. 

Saturday Oct 18th - First Round of the Big Sky Tournament to be played at Oasis Golf Club, a past course that displays great desert golf.  Tee times begin at 9:10 am.

 Upon completion of play we have booked additional tee times at 3:00 on the Vistas 9 course for those that would want to play 9 more holes which will be optional and not a part of the Big Sky Tournament, but John Graves has promised to come up with a "game" to make it more interesting for those that participate.  

I could also be persuaded to book massages for those that would prefer to go this more relaxing route your choice. 

Sunday Oct 19th - First thing in the morning pack up cars to be ready to depart Mesquite.

Second Round of Big Sky Tournament to be played at Wolf Creek, another past played course that retains it's magnificence. Tee times begin at 10:00 am.

 When we finish playing golf, eating lunch, and paying out the day money. We will load up and head back to Vegas, where we have reservations at Bally's for Sunday and Monday nights. I have agreed to a service that I hope everyone will be ok with. For $4.50 a person they will provide group baggage handling, which means they will carry our bags to our rooms when we check in, and from the room to the lobby at checkout. This charge will also assist in getting us into the newly renovated tower at Bally's (not a guarantee, but should assist).

 Monday October 20th - Third Round of the Big Sky tournament to be played at Silverstone Golf Club just outside Vegas, new course that from the website appears to have similarities to Paiute but with a better price tag. Tee times begin at 9:00 am 

We will plan dinner at a Vegas Sports Bar, so that the group can view Monday Night Football.

       

Catch Planes back to home on Tuesday Oct 21st. Pat Buckley has suggested another round of golf on Tuesday since a day of vacation will already be burned. But we will work on this once we are sure Pat will be there this year !!!!
